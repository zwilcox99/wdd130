Trips

Rogue River Falls

This adventure challenges a team of four rafters against a seemingly calm river that quickly turns rapid as you approach a series of short falls. Come raft with us down this 4.5-mile run on the Rogue River! Typically best for new rafters.

Snake Bend Adventure

Find yourself rafting down the Colorado River as you face the twists and turns of the Grand Canyon. Small teams of two to four semi-skilled adventureres are typically best for this route.

Rainforest Run

Join us as we travel down a river that runs right through the center of the Indonesian rainforest. This experience is for seasoned rafters who are looking for a daring adventure through the turbulent waters of Indonesia.
